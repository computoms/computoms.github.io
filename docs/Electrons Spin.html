Electrons Spin

An intrinsic property of electrons and sub-atomic particles.

The spin of particles, an intrinsic property of sub-atomic particles such as electrons and protons/neutrons, is responsible for the magnetic orientation of atoms. The magnetic orientation is also called the magnetic moment.

The spin is the property of sub-atomic elementary particles that is responsible for the magnetic force and for the properties related to magnets.
